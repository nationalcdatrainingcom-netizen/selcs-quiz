Understanding SELCS

The Social Emotional Leadership Communication Style (SELCS) framework identifies four core orientations that shape how individuals communicate, lead, and build relationships in early childhood education settings.

Every person carries elements of all four styles. Many people are a blend of two dominant styles — for example, a Driver-Inspirer or a Sustainer-Cultivator. The SELCS assessment reveals your unique profile, whether pure or blended, empowering you to mentor more effectively.
