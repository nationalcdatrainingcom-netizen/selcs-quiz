Framework

Understanding SELCS

The Social Emotional Leadership Communication Style (SELCS) framework identifies four distinct temperament orientations that shape how individuals communicate, lead, and build relationships in early childhood settings.

Every person carries elements of all four styles — the SELCS assessment reveals your unique blend and identifies which orientations are most and least dominant. Understanding your profile empowers you to mentor more effectively by appreciating the diverse styles of those you lead.
